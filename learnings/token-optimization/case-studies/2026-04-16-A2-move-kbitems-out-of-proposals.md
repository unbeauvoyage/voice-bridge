## What changed

Created `~/environment/knowledge-base-exports/` directory. Moved 76 files matching `2026-04-14-kb-item-*`, `2026-04-14-kb-raise-*`, `2026-04-14-kb-test-*`, and `2026-04-14-kb-history-*` from `proposals/` to this new directory.

Added `README.md` to knowledge-base-exports/ explaining the directory purpose.

## Before / After

| Metric | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| proposals/ file count | ~148 | ~72 |
| Auto-generated files in proposals/ | 76 | 0 |
| knowledge-base-exports/ | n/a | 77 items (76 exports + README) |

## Decision rationale

The `proposals/` directory is designed for human decision proposals — CEO reads/approves them. The 76 `kb-item-*` and `kb-raise-*` files were auto-generated by the knowledge-base service during testing on 2026-04-14. They were never proposals — they were KB service test artifacts.

These files:
1. Polluted proposal scans (agents scanning proposals/ for decisions got 76 irrelevant files)
2. Added ~22k tokens of content per scan for zero value
3. Made it impossible to visually scan proposals/ for real decisions

Cross-reference: Anti-Pattern AP3 (Auto-generated content in a human-reviewed directory).

## Tokens saved

- ~22k tokens per proposals/ scan (76 files × avg 290 lines × ~10 tokens/line)
- Proposals/ is scanned by agents on every session start

## Reversibility

Fully reversible: `mv knowledge-base-exports/2026-04-14-kb-* proposals/`. The files are identical — nothing was modified, only moved.

## Gotchas

- The `2026-04-14-kb-history-fixture-897622.md` file was also caught by the pattern even though it doesn't have `-item-` or `-raise-` in the name. Correctly moved — it's also a KB test artifact.
- Future KB service exports should be directed to `knowledge-base-exports/` directly, not `proposals/`. This needs a config change in the knowledge-base service (backlog item).
