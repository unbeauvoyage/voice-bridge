Research completed 2026-04-09. Sources: ttg.co.jp, smarite.co.jp, japantoday.com, gachagachaguide.com, biz.moneyforward.com, aucfan.com, pref.nara.jp.

**Why:** CEO evaluating vending machine route business in Nara/Osaka/Kyoto/Wakayama as TYPE A passive income.
**How to apply:** Use these numbers as baseline for financial modeling and location strategy.

## Revenue by Location (Drink Machines)
- National average: ~¥40,000/month gross sales
- Tourist spot / station front (premium): ¥300K–¥500K/month gross (top end, Shinjuku-class)
- Tourist spot (realistic Nara/Kyoto small shrine): ¥50K–¥150K/month gross
- Busy street / downtown commercial: ¥30K–¥50K/month
- Office building / hospital: ¥20K–¥40K/month
- Residential: ¥5K–¥15K/month
- Net owner profit (independent): 10–20% of gross after all costs

## Machine Capital Costs
- New beverage machine: ¥800K–¥1.5M
- Used (Yahoo Auctions): ¥200K–¥500K; Sanden avg auction price ¥193,217
- Used (Jimoty/local): ¥50K–¥300K
- Monthly lease: ¥19,800–¥50,000/month
- Lease is more expensive than buying used at 24+ months

## Operator Models
- Full operator (beverage company owns/manages machine): Location host gets 10–30% revenue. Zero work.
- Independent (セルフオペレーション): Owner buys machine, stocks own products, keeps ~20–30% gross margin. After location fee (¥10K flat or 30% of profits), electricity (¥2K–¥5K/month), depreciation, net ~10–20%.
- Break-even floor: ¥20,000/month gross sales minimum

## Market Structure
- Coca-Cola: ~980,000 machines nationally, requires 300–400 bottles/month minimum
- Suntory: ~490,000 machines, requires 500–600 bottles/month minimum
- Locations failing these thresholds are OPEN to independent operators
- Independent location fee: ¥10K/month flat OR 30% of profits
- Nara Prefecture runs public tender (公募) for park vending machine placement

## Gashapon Economics
- Machine cost: ¥20K–¥100K used; ¥100K new
- At ¥200/play, 10 units/day: ¥60K gross → ¥6K net (70% COGS + 20% location fee)
- At ¥400/play, 200 units/month: ¥80K gross → ¥40K net (30% COGS specialty products + 20% location fee)
- No electricity cost (mechanical)
- Tourist-specific/licensed products at ¥400–¥500 price points are key to profitability
- Standard gashapon margins are NOT better than drinks; specialty tourist gashapon are

## Legal
- No license for pre-packaged drinks/snacks
- Alcohol: requires 酒類販売業免許
- Business Manager Visa: requires ¥5M capital + 2 employees — vending route alone unlikely to qualify
- Tax: standard income tax for residents; 20% withholding for non-residents

## Break-Even (3-machine tourist route, ¥60K gross each)
- Monthly costs: ~¥128K
- Monthly revenue: ¥180K
- Net: ~¥52K/month
- To hit ¥100K net: 6 machines at ¥60K gross OR 3 machines at high-traffic spots (¥100K+ gross)
