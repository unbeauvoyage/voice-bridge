No formal route sale marketplace exists in Japan for small operators. Route sales are informal and rare. Tranbi/Batonz M&A platforms confirmed: no vending machine routes listed as of April 2026.

Yahoo Auctions avg for Fuji Electric drink machines: ~¥101,943. Operational units ¥100K–¥180K.

Jimoty (local classifieds) gashapon commercial columns: ¥20K–¥50K used. This is the real TYPE C play — cheap machine acquisition, not route buyouts.

Kansai-based gashapon placement operators: Kobe Cosmos (kobe-cosmos.co.jp) and Sanyo Apple (sanyo-apple.com) — both Kansai-based, do consignment placements at tourist locations, pay location ~10-20% commission.

Nara Park: Deer cracker machines are a corporate consortium (Dydo + Nara Visitors Bureau). Independent placement feasible at smaller souvenir shops and smaller temples, not major park structures.

Kyoto: Independent temple gashapon confirmed working (Ryuganji temple, Sokujo-in). Direct approach with location-specific content is the path.

JR/Kintetsu train stations: NOT accessible to independent operators.

**Why:** Informs capital planning and sourcing strategy — used gashapon at ¥20K–¥50K dramatically reduces startup capital vs drink machine routes at ¥100K+ per unit.
**How to apply:** When advising on capital requirements or TYPE C sourcing, recommend Jimoty/Yahoo Auctions gashapon columns, not drink machine route buyouts.
