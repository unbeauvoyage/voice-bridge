# Agency: Business Opportunities in Japan

See `~/.claude/CLAUDE-common-to-all-agency-projects.md` for common rules.

## Mission
Research and catalog ALL viable business opportunities in Japan, with focus on Kansai (Nara, Kyoto, Osaka). CEO advises customers on what businesses to start in Japan.

## Scope
- Physical: retail, food/drink, services, tourism, trades
- Import/export and trading opportunities
- Digital/online as secondary focus
- Nara first, then Kyoto, Osaka, Tokyo, Nagoya

## Per-Opportunity Template
Business type · Why Japan/Kansai · Startup costs (JPY) · Legal requirements · Competition · Profit potential · Best locations · Real examples · Risks · Sources
