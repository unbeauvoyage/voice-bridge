## Mission
Find the best opportunities and locations for opening a small Turkish kabab buffet shop in the Kansai region. Ongoing market scanning.

## Priority
1. **Nara** (primary)
2. **Kyoto** (secondary)
3. **Osaka** (tertiary)

## What to Research
- Available small retail/food stall spaces (rent prices, location, foot traffic, size)
- Existing Turkish/kebab/döner shops: where are they? How are they doing? Pricing?
- Gap analysis: areas with tourist traffic but no kebab options
- Buffet concept: small-format all-you-can-eat kebab, feasibility, pricing models
- Halal food market in Kansai: Muslim tourist numbers, halal certification requirements
- Costs: rent, equipment (döner machines, grills), licenses, staffing, meat supply chain
- Legal: food service licenses, halal certification process, health regulations
- Supplier research: halal meat suppliers in Kansai region
- Nara advantages: tourist flow near temples/deer park, lower rent
- Competition from konbini/fast food: price point comparison
- Seasonal patterns: tourist seasons, Ramadan considerations
