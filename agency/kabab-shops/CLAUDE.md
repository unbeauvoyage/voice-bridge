# Agency: Turkish Kabab Buffet — Nara / Kyoto / Osaka

See `~/.claude/CLAUDE-common-to-all-agency-projects.md` for common rules.

## Mission
Find opportunities and locations for a small Turkish kabab buffet in Kansai. Priority: Nara → Kyoto → Osaka.

## Key Research Areas
- Available retail/food stall spaces (rent, location, foot traffic, size)
- Existing Turkish/kebab shops: locations, pricing, gap analysis
- Buffet feasibility: small-format all-you-can-eat kabab pricing models
- Halal food market: Muslim tourist numbers, certification requirements
- Costs: rent, döner machines/grills, licenses, staffing, halal meat supply
- Nara advantages: tourist flow, lower rent; seasonal patterns
