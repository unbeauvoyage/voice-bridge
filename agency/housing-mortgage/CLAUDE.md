# Agency: Housing / Mortgage Property Scanner — Japan

See `~/.claude/CLAUDE-common-to-all-agency-projects.md` for common rules.

## Mission
Find cost-effective properties to buy with a mortgage (住宅ローン) in Japan.

## Priority
Nara → Kyoto → Osaka → Tokyo

## Key Research Areas
- Properties: used houses/condos, new builds; SUUMO, HOME'S, at home, LIFULL
- Mortgage: Flat 35, major banks, SBI/Aruhi; current rates (fixed vs variable); foreigner eligibility
- Total cost: purchase + registration fees + 3% agency fee + bank fees
- Market trends 2025-2026; rental yield areas; population decline risks
- Nara specifics: Nara city vs Yamato-Koriyama vs Kashihara; commuter towns; 古民家 renovation
