Researched 2026-04-08. Full findings returned to parent. Key numbers:

- Foreign buyers: ~27% of transactions nationwide, up to 40% of new Tokyo apartments
- Only 10.5% of foreigners living in Japan have bought property
- 60% of foreign residents describe property info access as "bad/very bad"
- Chinese nationals = 40% of foreign buyers at Tokyo agencies; 47.5% near sensitive facilities
- Flat 35: ~2.08% (Jan 2026, 21-35yr loans); variable rates moving toward 0.6-0.75%
- Foreigners without PR: typically need 20-30% down, LTV capped ~80%
- New nationality disclosure law: from fiscal 2026 (April 2026), buyers must declare nationality at registration
- Nara land: ~78,300 yen/sqm (2023); 74.1% homeownership rate
- Osaka new condo average: ¥55-57M; secondhand 70sqm condo ~¥18-20M
- Kyoto new condo average: ~¥60M

**Why:** Informs housing advisory business model targeting foreign/expat buyers in Kansai.
**How to apply:** Use these numbers as baseline for subsequent market analysis and service gap research.
