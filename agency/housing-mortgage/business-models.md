# Kansai Housing Passive Income — Business Models

Three distinct customer archetypes, each with a tailored product offering and revenue path. Geographic focus: Nara, Kyoto, Osaka — with Wakayama arbitrage option.

---

## Customer Archetype A — Minimal Capital (¥500K–¥2M budget)

**Profile:** Digital nomad, remote worker, expat on temporary visa, or young Japanese starting investor. Cannot meet ¥5.65M full-acquisition threshold.

### Model 1 — Lease & Sublease (Akiya Management)
- Lease distressed akiya (空き家 / vacant house) at ¥0 from desperate owner
- Renovate with personal capital ¥500K–¥1M
- Sublet rooms at ¥40–50K/month per room
- Pocket the spread; no ownership risk

### Model 2 — Fractional Co-Ownership
- Pool capital with 2–3 co-investors, each ¥1.5–2M
- Joint purchase of ¥5.65M property (3-person partnership structure)
- Share rental income ¥75K/month → ~¥25K per investor
- **~13%+ annual return** on invested capital

### Product Offering — "Akiya Co-Ownership Fund"
- ¥2M minimum entry
- Turnkey: we source, acquire, renovate, place tenants, distribute income
- Investor receives monthly deposit + appreciation share

---

## Customer Archetype B — Medium Capital (¥3M–¥8M budget)

**Profile:** Tokyo/Osaka professional considering relocation or investment; expat with savings; business owner diversifying.

### Model — Solo Acquisition + Passive PM
- Single distressed akiya (~¥5.65M total cost including fees)
- ¥1–2M personal capital + mortgage financing (住宅ローン / Flat 35 or regional bank)
- Long-term rental via property management — truly passive
- **Annual passive income: ¥250–350K**

### Product Offering — Full Turnkey Advisory
- Sourcing → acquisition → renovation → PM coordination
- **Flat fee:** ¥300K–500K for complete turnkey service
- **OR ongoing:** 2–3% of annual rent for managed relationship

---

## Customer Archetype C — Large Capital (¥10M+ budget)

**Profile:** Scale-minded investor, family office, corporate buyer seeking Japan real estate exposure.

### Model — Portfolio Construction
- Build 3–5 property portfolio
- Target distressed deals + court auctions (競売 / keibai) for highest ROI
- Full leverage: ~10% down per property
- **Potential annual income: ¥750K–1M+** across portfolio

### Product Offering — Institutional-Grade Service
- Portfolio construction: source 3–5 properties, negotiate bulk discounts
- Consortium PM rate (2% vs. individual 5%)
- Tax optimization across portfolio (depreciation laddering)
- Exit planning: refinance, inheritance structuring (相続対策)

## Competitive Advantages

1. **English-language service** — most Kansai competitors are Japanese-only
2. **Distressed deal sourcing** — court auctions (競売), expat/divorce networks, rural akiya banks
3. **Tax optimization for foreign buyers** — FEFTA compliance, depreciation strategies
4. **PM coordination model** — we don't manage directly; we coordinate local PMs and keep the spread
5. **Multi-prefecture arbitrage** — Nara / Wakayama / Osaka price-to-yield differentials
