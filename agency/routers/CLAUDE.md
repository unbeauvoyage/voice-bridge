# Agency: Router Research — Japan

See `~/.claude/CLAUDE-common-to-all-agency-projects.md` for common rules.

## Mission
Research the best internet routers available in Japan. Comprehensive, data-driven market overview.

## Key Research Areas
- Top models (Wi-Fi 6, 6E, 7); ISP compatibility (NTT, KDDI, SoftBank, docomo, NURO)
- Price ranges: budget to high-end; where to buy (Amazon JP, Yodobashi, Bic Camera)
- Performance: speeds, range, stability, latency; mesh systems popular in Japan
- Router vs ONT/HGW: when to use ISP equipment vs own
- Consumer sentiment: kakaku.com reviews, Japanese tech forums
- Best value picks by use case: apartment, house, gaming, WFH
