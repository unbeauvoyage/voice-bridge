# Agency: Coin Parking — Kansai Japan

See `~/.claude/CLAUDE-common-to-all-agency-projects.md` for common rules.

## Mission
Build an actionable business model for a coin parking lot (コインパーキング) in Kansai. TYPE A passive: 3–10 spaces, machines collect 24/7, owner visits weekly. Target: ¥150K–¥400K/month.

## Key Research Areas
- Revenue per space in Nara, Osaka, near hospitals/stations; occupancy rates
- Machine costs: flat vs gate systems; companies (Times24, Navipark, D-Parking)
- Foreigner ownership; permits in Nara/Osaka
- Land lease costs per tsubo; finding vacant lots
- Self-managed vs managed service (revenue split)
- TYPE C: operators exiting business, discounted locations
- Nara-specific: tourist demand near Nara Park, Todai-ji, seasonal spikes
