# Agency: Trunk Room / Self-Storage — Kansai Japan

See `~/.claude/CLAUDE-common-to-all-agency-projects.md` for common rules.

## Mission
Build an actionable business model for a trunk room (トランクルーム) in Kansai (Nara, Osaka, Kyoto, Wakayama). TYPE A/B passive income: 20–50 units at ¥5K–¥15K/unit/month, 70-80% occupancy = ¥700K–¥600K/month on ¥3M–¥5M investment.

## Key Research Areas
- Unit rent rates and occupancy in Nara/Osaka; break-even timeline
- Franchise vs independent (Quraz, Trunk Box, MoveIn, Hello Storage)
- Foreigner ownership; licensing requirements
- Building types: warehouse, container, parking garage conversions
- Smart lock/access automation systems and costs
- TYPE C: existing businesses for sale, warehouse conversion partnerships
