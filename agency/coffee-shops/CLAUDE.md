# Agency: Coffee Shop / Buffet Scanner — Nara / Kyoto / Osaka

See `~/.claude/CLAUDE-common-to-all-agency-projects.md` for common rules.

## Mission
Find opportunities and locations for a coffee shop or coffee buffet in Kansai. Priority: Nara → Kyoto → Osaka.

## Key Research Areas
- Available retail spaces: rent, location, foot traffic, size
- Coffee shop density: gaps in coverage, underserved neighborhoods
- Coffee buffet concept: all-you-can-drink models, pricing, existing examples in Japan
- Costs: rent, equipment, licenses, staffing, bean supply chain
- Revenue estimates by area; competitor analysis
- Nara advantages: lower rent, tourist traffic; seasonal patterns
