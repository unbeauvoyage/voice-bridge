**See `~/.claude/CLAUDE-common-to-all-agency-projects.md`** for common agency rules: research methodology, communication patterns, role definitions. This file contains market-specific content only.

## Mission
Find the best opportunities and locations for opening a coffee shop or coffee buffet in the Kansai region. Ongoing market scanning.

## Priority
1. **Nara** (primary)
2. **Kyoto** (secondary)
3. **Osaka** (tertiary)

## What to Research
- Available retail spaces for rent (rent prices, location, foot traffic, size)
- Existing coffee shop landscape: density, gaps in coverage, underserved neighborhoods
- Tourist foot traffic patterns: which areas get consistent tourist flow?
- Competitor analysis: pricing, concept, what's working/failing
- Coffee buffet concept specifically: all-you-can-drink coffee bars, existing ones in Japan, pricing models
- Costs: rent, equipment, licenses, staffing, supply chain for beans
- Legal: food service licenses in each city, health regulations, business registration
- Revenue estimates: average coffee shop revenue in each area
- Nara advantages: lower rent than Kyoto/Osaka, tourist traffic from deer park area
- Seasonal patterns: tourist seasons, local events that drive traffic
